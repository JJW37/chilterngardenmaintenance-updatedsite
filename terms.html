Terms and Conditions

Last updated: January 2026

These terms and conditions govern the provision of garden services by Chiltern Garden Maintenance ("we", "us", "our") to you ("the client"). By engaging our services, you agree to these terms.

1. Services

We provide garden maintenance, clearance, soft landscaping, pressure washing, hedge cutting, lawn recovery and related services. The specific services to be provided will be agreed in writing before work commences.

2. Quotes and Pricing

All quotes are valid for 30 days unless otherwise stated. Quotes are based on the information available at the time of quoting. If additional work is required that was not foreseeable at the time of quoting, we will discuss the additional cost with you before proceeding.

Estimates provided through our website calculators are guide ranges only and are not binding. Final pricing depends on access, waste, ground conditions, material choice, urgency and site complexity.

3. Payment

Payment is due within 7 days of completion of the work unless otherwise agreed in writing. For regular maintenance, we offer monthly direct debit. We accept bank transfer, cash and major debit cards.

Late payment may incur interest at 8 percent above the Bank of England base rate, plus compensation under the Late Payment of Commercial Debts (Interest) Act 1998 where applicable.

4. Cancellation

You may cancel a booking by giving us at least 48 hours notice. Cancellations with less than 48 hours notice may incur a charge of 50 percent of the agreed price. Regular maintenance can be cancelled at any time with 30 days notice.

5. Access

You agree to provide safe access to the work area, including parking for our vehicles if required. You must inform us of any known hazards including asbestos, unsafe structures or aggressive animals.

6. Property and Damage

We carry public liability insurance. We will take reasonable care of your property during the work. Any damage caused by our negligence will be rectified or compensated. We are not liable for pre-existing damage or for damage caused by concealed hazards that were not disclosed to us.

7. Waste

We take all waste away from every job. We are unable to remove asbestos or hazardous chemicals. These require specialist contractors.

8. Wildlife

We will not cut hedges or prune shrubs during the bird nesting season (1 March to 31 August) if active nests are present. We will advise you if we need to delay work for this reason.

9. Tree Preservation Orders

If your property has trees with a Tree Preservation Order or is in a conservation area, you are responsible for obtaining any necessary permissions before work commences. We can advise on the process.

10. Weather

We reserve the right to postpone work due to adverse weather conditions. We will rearrange the work at the earliest convenient date.

11. Variations

Any variation to these terms must be agreed in writing. Our employees and contractors are not authorised to vary these terms verbally.

12. Governing Law

These terms are governed by the laws of England and Wales. Any disputes will be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the English courts.

13. Contact

If you have any questions about these terms, please contact us.
