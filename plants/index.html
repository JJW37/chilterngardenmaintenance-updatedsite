Chiltern Plant Library

A working library of 130 plants we use in real gardens across the Chilterns and Thames Valley. Each profile includes light, soil, pruning, wildlife value and our own CGM care notes.

About this library

This is a working library updated by our gardeners, not an encyclopaedia. Every plant here is one of many we have planted, pruned or maintained in real gardens across Oxfordshire, Buckinghamshire, Berkshire and Bedfordshire. The CGM care notes are our own observations from working with each plant in the Chilterns.
