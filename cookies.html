Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Chiltern Garden Maintenance uses cookies and similar technologies on this website. By using this website, you agree to the use of cookies as described in this policy.

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites you visit. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently and to provide information to site owners.

Cookies We Use

Essential cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function. They remember your cookie consent preferences. Without these cookies, the website would not work properly.

  • cgm_cookie_consent: remembers whether you have accepted or rejected non-essential cookies. Stored for 12 months.

Analytics cookies (only if you accept)

We use Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors use our website. This helps us improve content and fix problems. Analytics only runs after you click "Accept" on our cookie banner. If you click "Decline", no analytics data is sent to Google.

Google Analytics sets the following cookies when you accept:

  • _ga: a random anonymous ID that distinguishes you from other visitors. Stored for 2 years.
  • _ga_<container-id>: maintains session state for our property. Stored for 2 years.

We have enabled IP anonymisation, so Google never sees your full IP address. Google Analytics does not track you across other websites, does not show you personalised advertising, and does not share data with third parties for advertising purposes. You can read Google's own guidance on how Analytics uses data.

You can withdraw consent at any time by clearing your browser's local storage for our domain, which removes the cgm_cookie_consent flag and shows the cookie banner again on your next visit.

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • See what cookies are stored
  • Delete individual cookies
  • Block all cookies
  • Allow only trusted sites to set cookies

Blocking all cookies may affect the functionality of this and other websites.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
