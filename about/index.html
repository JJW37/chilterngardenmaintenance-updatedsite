About Chiltern Garden Maintenance

A real local business working across the Chilterns and Thames Valley. Reliable, qualified and properly insured.

Who We Are

Chiltern Garden Maintenance is a small, locally based garden and grounds maintenance business working across Oxfordshire, Buckinghamshire, Berkshire and Bedfordshire. We look after residential and small commercial gardens of all sizes, from courtyard spaces to substantial family gardens and rural properties.

We are not a franchise, a call centre, or a gardening app. When you contact us, you speak directly to the people who will do the work. We turn up when we say we will, we carry proper insurance, and we leave every garden cleaner than we found it.

What We Do

Our services cover the full range of garden work:

  • Regular garden maintenance visits
  • One-off garden clearance
  • Soft landscaping and planting schemes
  • Pressure washing of hard surfaces
  • Hedge cutting and pruning
  • Lawn recovery and renovation
  • Handyman work and small outdoor repairs

Our Service Area

We cover a 25 mile radius from our base in the Chilterns. The towns we work in most often include High Wycombe, Aylesbury, Oxford, Reading, Henley-on-Thames, Marlow, Beaconsfield, Amersham, Berkhamsted and Princes Risborough. See our location pages for town-specific information on local soil, rainfall and garden types.

Our Approach

We believe garden care should be honest, knowledgeable and consistent. We turn up when we say we will. We tell you what your garden needs, not what we want to sell. We carry insurance and we take all waste away from every clearance. We treat every garden as if it were our own.

For more detail on how we approach regular maintenance, see our CGM Method page.

Get In Touch

If you would like to talk to us about your garden, the best first step is a few photos via WhatsApp or a quick message through our quote form. We respond within 12 to 36 hours, Monday to Sunday.
