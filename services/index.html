The CGM Method

Our method is built on four principles

01

Right plant, right place

A plant in the wrong place will always struggle. We choose plants that suit the soil, light and moisture of each spot in the garden.

02

Right time, right job

Every gardening job has a right time of year. We do things when they will be most effective, not when they are most convenient.

03

Prevention over cure

A mulch in spring saves hours of weeding in summer. A regular feed keeps plants healthier than a panic feed when they are struggling.

04

Honest advice

If a plant is past saving, we will say so. If a job does not need doing, we will say so. If a cheaper option exists, we will tell you about it.
