Garden Clearance

Full clearance of overgrown gardens, brambles, ivy and old structures.

What Our Garden Clearance Includes

Whether your garden has got away from you over a few seasons, or you have just moved into a property with a jungle out the back, our clearance service gets it back to a blank canvas. We do the lot, including the waste.

  • Cutting back all overgrowth to ground level
  • Removal of brambles, ivy and self-seeded weeds
  • Digging out invasive root systems where requested
  • Pruning of shrubs to a manageable size
  • Removal of small trees (under 5 metres)
  • Shed and outbuilding demolition (non-asbestos)
  • Removal of all green waste
  • Sweeping and basic tidy of hard surfaces
Waste Removal

We take all waste away from every job. The garden is left clean and tidy.

What We Cannot Take

There are a few things we cannot take and which need specialist contractors:

  • Asbestos: if you have an old shed or garage with an asbestos cement roof, it needs a licensed asbestos contractor. We can recommend one and pause the clearance while they do their part.
  • Hazardous chemicals: old paint, pesticides, oil etc need to go to a civic amenity site for specialist disposal.
  • Trees with preservation orders: if a tree on your property has a TPO, you need council permission before any work. We can advise and help with the application.
Get a Quote

The quickest way to get a price is to send us a few photos of your garden via WhatsApp or email, along with your postcode and a brief description of what you need. We will review the photos and get back to you with a quote or arrange a free site visit if needed.

FAQs
Is this a one-off service?

Yes, it is usually a one-off clearance or recovery service. Many customers then move onto Regular Garden Maintenance.

Can you clear a very overgrown garden?

Usually yes, subject to access and site conditions. Send photos first for an accurate assessment.

Can pressure washing be included?

Yes, in many cases paths or patios can be included or quoted separately.

Do I need to be home?

Not always, as long as access and priorities are agreed in advance.

Is a deposit required?

Yes, an initial deposit is usually required for this service to secure a booking date.
