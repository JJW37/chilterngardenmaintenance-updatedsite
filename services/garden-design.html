Garden Design

Bespoke garden design plans, planting schemes and full redesigns.

Our Garden Design Services

Whether you want a full garden redesign or a planting plan for a single border, our garden design service gives you a clear, actionable plan. We work with the soil, light and conditions of your garden rather than against them, choosing plants that will thrive in your specific spot.

  • Full garden design plans, from survey to planting
  • Planting schemes for new or existing borders
  • Garden zoning and layout plans
  • Low-maintenance garden design
  • Wildlife-friendly garden design
  • Privacy and screening design
  • Entertaining and outdoor living design
  • Border restoration plans for tired gardens
Our Design Process
  1. Site survey: we measure the garden, note sun and shade, take soil samples, and photograph the existing features
  2. Brief: we discuss how you use the garden, what you want from it, and your budget
  3. Concept plan: a scaled drawing showing the layout, zones and key features
  4. Planting plan: a detailed plan showing plant species, quantities and positions
  5. Quote: a fixed price for implementing the design
  6. Implementation: we carry out the work, or you can use the plan to do it yourself or with another contractor
Design Styles

We design in a range of styles to suit your property and taste:

  • Cottage garden: informal, densely planted, traditional English style
  • Contemporary: clean lines, architectural plants, minimal palette
  • Wildlife-friendly: native plants, pollinator focus, year-round interest
  • Low-maintenance: evergreen structure, ground cover, minimal lawn
  • Mediterranean: drought-tolerant plants, gravel, silver foliage
  • Formal: symmetry, structure, topiary, defined beds
Chiltern Guides for Garden Design

If you want to design your own garden, we have a range of Chiltern Garden Design Plan PDF guides covering cottage, pollinator, English, wildflower, topiary, modern tropical, minimalist and year-round blooming designs. Each guide includes a planting plan and layout you can adapt to your garden.

FAQs
Do you provide a full design service?

Yes, from site survey through to planting plan and implementation.

How much does a garden design cost?

Send us photos of your garden via WhatsApp or email and we will provide a quote based on what you need.

Can you work with existing plants?

Yes, we assess existing plants and incorporate the ones worth keeping into the new design.

Do you provide aftercare?

Yes, we can provide ongoing maintenance to keep the garden looking its best after the design is implemented.

Can I implement the design myself?

Yes, you can buy the design plan and implement it yourself, or hire us to do the work.
