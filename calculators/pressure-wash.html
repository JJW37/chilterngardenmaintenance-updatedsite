Pressure Washing Level Assessment

Answer a few questions about the area you want cleaned. We will assess which level of pressure washing is needed. Levels range from Level 1 (Light Clean) to Level 4 (Major Surface Recovery). The actual level may change once we assess the area in person. For a fixed quote, contact us for a free site visit.

How this tool works

This tool gives you tailored guidance based on your inputs. Some tools assess a level (Level 1 to 4), others recommend specific seed mixes, plant combinations or give a recovery score. No prices, hours or fixed quotes are shown. The results are a starting point to help you understand what to expect. The actual scope and price can only be confirmed after we see the site in person. Use the result as a starting point, then send us a few photos for an accurate, fixed price.
