What Happens After You Contact Us

  1. Initial response: we typically respond within 12 to 36 hours, Monday to Sunday, to find out more about the problem you face.
  2. Photos: if you have not already sent them, we ask for a few photos of the garden to survey the problems in greater detail.
  3. Site visit: we will arrange a free in person visit to confirm the work.
  4. Quotation: we will get back in touch, and send to you a free in depth quote listing out our proposal for the work.
  5. Booking: we agree a date and prepare for the work.
  6. Work: we arrive on the agreed date and complete the work.
